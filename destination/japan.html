Discover Japan

Explore the vibrant city of Japan, South Africa’s economic hub.

Popular Attractions

  • Mount Fuji: Japan’s iconic volcano and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Kyoto Temples: Explore beautiful historic temples like Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion).
  • Tokyo Skytree: Japan's tallest tower offering stunning city views.
  • Ueno Park: Famous for cherry blossoms in spring and cultural museums.
  • Hiroshima Peace Memorial: A moving tribute to Hiroshima’s history and resilience.
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine: Walk through the vibrant red torii gates.

Cultural Highlights

Japan blends ancient traditions with modern innovations. Tea ceremonies, kabuki theater, and sumo wrestling offer glimpses into Japan’s heritage, while cherry blossom festivals celebrate nature’s beauty. Shinto shrines and Buddhist temples coexist alongside futuristic architecture, reflecting the country’s cultural harmony.
