Experience Zambia

Popular Attractions

  • Victoria Falls: See the "Smoke That Thunders" from the thrilling Zambian side
  • South Luangwa National Park: Renowned for walking safaris and abundant wildlife
  • Lower Zambezi National Park: Explore the Zambezi River by canoe or boat safari
  • Lake Tanganyika: Africa's deepest lake, perfect for diving and relaxation
  • Kafue National Park: One of Africa's largest parks, untouched and wild
  • Livingstone: A charming town with adventure sports, museums, and cultural sites

Cultural Highlights

Experience vibrant village life, traditional ceremonies like the Kuomboka Festival, and the warmth of Zambian hospitality as you journey through this incredible land of diverse cultures and traditions.

Discover the rich heritage of Zambia's 73 ethnic groups, experience traditional music and dance performances, and learn about the country's journey to independence and its role in regional politics.
