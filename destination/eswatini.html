Discover eSwatini

The Kingdom of eSwatini (formerly Swaziland) - Africa's last absolute monarchy, where ancient traditions and breathtaking landscapes await

Experience eSwatini

Popular Attractions

  • Mlilwane Wildlife Sanctuary: eSwatini's oldest protected area, perfect for hiking, mountain biking, and seeing zebras, wildebeest, and antelopes up close
  • Ezulwini Valley: Known as the "Valley of Heaven," home to luxury resorts, craft markets, and the famous Mantenga Cultural Village
  • Hlane Royal National Park: See lions, elephants, and white rhinos in this vast lowland reserve managed by the King of eSwatini
  • Malolotja Nature Reserve: Stunning mountain scenery with hiking trails, waterfalls, and the longest canopy tour in Africa
  • Mbabane: The charming capital city with vibrant markets, restaurants, and panoramic mountain views
  • Ngwenya Glass Village: Watch artisans create beautiful recycled glass pieces using traditional methods

Cultural Highlights

eSwatini is renowned for preserving its rich cultural heritage. The annual Umhlanga (Reed Dance) ceremony brings together thousands of unmarried Swazi maidens dancing before the King. The Incwala (First Fruits) ceremony is the kingdom's most sacred event, celebrating the harvest season and royal power.

Visit traditional Swazi villages to witness vibrant dance performances, intricate beadwork, and basket weaving. The Swazi people are known for their warm hospitality, colorful attire, and deep respect for their monarchy, which has remained unbroken for generations.
