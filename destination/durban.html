Popular Attractions

  • uShaka Marine World: A family-friendly marine park with aquariums and water rides.
  • Golden Mile Beaches: Stretch of sun-soaked beaches perfect for swimming and surfing.
  • Moses Mabhida Stadium: An architectural marvel with panoramic views.
  • Durban Botanic Gardens: Africa's oldest surviving botanical gardens.
  • Victoria Street Market: A bustling market filled with spices, crafts, and souvenirs.
  • Umgeni River Bird Park: A serene sanctuary with exotic bird species.

Cultural Highlights

Durban is a melting pot of Zulu, Indian, and colonial influences. Visitors can enjoy Zulu cultural villages, taste authentic Indian cuisine, and explore historical landmarks like the Juma Mosque and Victoria Street Market.
